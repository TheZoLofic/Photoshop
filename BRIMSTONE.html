1. BRIMSTONE





country of origin : united states of america

   Joining from the USA, Brimstone's orbital arsenal ensures his squad
   always has the advantage. His ability to deliver utility precisely and
  from a distance make him an unmatched boots-on-the-ground commander.

HUMAN

ROLE:  CONTROLLER

BASIC ABILITIES: INCENDIARY, STIM BEACON

SIGNATURE ABILITIES:  SKY SMOKE

ULTIMATE ABILITY(ULT.): ORBITAL STRIKE

ULT. POINTS:  7



BRIMSTONE'S ABILITIES


BASIC ABILITIES

1. STIM BEACON - INSTANTLY toss down a stim beacon. Upon landing, it creates a field that grants players RapidFire and a speed boost. [C][1]

2. INCENDIARY- EQUIP an incendiary grenade launcher. FIRE to launch a grenade that detonates as it comes to a rest on the floor, creating a lingering fire zone that damages players within the zone. [Q][1]


SIGNATURE ABILITIES

3. SKY SMOKE- EQUIP a tactical map. FIRE to set locations where Brimstone's smoke clouds will land. ALTERNATE FIRE to confirm, launching long-lasting smoke clouds that block vision in the selected area. [E][3]

ULTIMATE ABILITY

4. ORBITAL STRIKE - EQUIP a tactical map. FIRE to launch a lingering orbital strike laser at the selected location, dealing high damage-over-time to players caught in the selected area. [X][1][7 points]
